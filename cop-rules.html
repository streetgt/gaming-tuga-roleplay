TROUBLESHOOTING AND NEW INTERNAL RULES 
 
 

Since there has been some trouble going here are some new and partly old rules you have to follow. Any violation will lead to a degradation to rank 1 or a kick from the Police Force.
  1. Every cadet stays at rank 1 for at least 1 week. No exceptions!
  2. If a chief or instructor does not fulfill his job correctly he won't hold this office after a 2nd warning.
  3. We will investigate about every single complaint. If there are several complaints from different players about one person, the person will be degraded.

  6. Do not run over suspects to kill them!
Courtesy. Professionalism. Respect. - Stick to that motto. You represent the state.


Rules for Police, FBI and National Guard


In this thread are the rules for the Police, FBI and the National Guard from the GT:SAMP RolePlay Server.

All well-known rules that are made before this thread, are not valid anymore!

1. Fundamental

A Police officer, a member of the FBI or a soldier from the National Guard (NG) are not allowed to harm someone that have wantedlevel 2 or less. The weapon is the last tool to use to solve something.

Every police officer, member of the FBI or soldier from the National Guard is allowed to use a vehicle that is able for their rank/job and take a colleague with him/her to go on patrol in San Andreas.

Recommended is 2 persons in 1 vehicle. One is driving and the other take care about the wantedlevels and megaphone messages etc..

2. The Units

2.1 The Policedepartments   

- Ensure for order in San Andreas
- Are a contact partner by every kind of questions
- Give the citizens of San Andreas a feeling to security and justice


2.2 The FBI   

- The FBI helps by obscure situations
- Pays attention by armed robberies
- Strike force and troop for special tasks of all kinds, requestable of the policy and the Special Forces


2.3 The National Guard   

- Retain peace and security in San Andreas and ensure their defense
- Support the national policy
- Protect the national rights
- Give special instructions to the Admins
- Protect the mayor personally in form of a body guard


3. Responsibility

3.1 Organization according to WantedLevels   

Police: From Wanted 1
FBI: From Wanted 5
NG: From WantedLevel 10, After permission from the FBI from WantedLevel 5


3.2 Geographic Organisation   

Never is a Police Officer from a Department allowed to come out of their district (On Duty).
No special arrangments for leaders or admins.

LVPD: District Las Venturas
SFPD: District San Fierro
LSPD: Distrikt Los Santos

It's not permitted to be active in another district.
The officers may cross the district line, when they are following suspects.
When they lost the suspect, they should go back to their district immediatly!

FBI: Entire area of the State of San Andreas

NG: Entire area of the State of San Andreas.


4. Behavior & Appearance

Fundamental   

Police Departments, FBI, National Guard - All these units represent the government authority, therefore members of these units always have to take themselves away accordingly, even if these are not in the service from their department.

Leaders that work for the goverment aren't permitted to become detective, hire one.


4.1 Movement   

As progressive movement means that the vehicles must always serve the respective unit during the office hours.

Also policemen and members FBI or the NG have to obay the traffic rules!
In the chase, with sirene (if available), may be given extra gas, unnecessary damages to property and person should be avoided.


4.2 Weapons   

It is open for members of the NG whether they carry their weapon openly with theirself or not.

Policemen and members of the FBI may show these only in dangerous situations (arrests, Robs, in prohibited areas etc.).

Only weapons in the service may be used which one can purchase in the police armoury .

The gun license can be made by each user starting from level 3.

When may the weapons be removed?
Players are permitted to carry a weapon. When the person shoot on a Policemen or other goverment worker, may the weapons be removed.


4.3 Behavior opposite civilians   

Members of the mentioned units should be always friendly and helpful opposite other players of the server, particularly if these are new.

Take civilians along in the official vehicle is not forbidden, however this should not be the case during the whole employment.

6. Traffic controls

Fundamental   

Responsibly: All Policedepartments (LS, SF and LVPD)


6.1 General control   


If a vehicle is sighted, it may be stopped, even if the driver did not commit a criminal offence. If the driver obayed the traffic rules, than the policeman has to control only the Licenses and passport.

When examining, the following must be considered:

To including WantedLevel 2, the driver has the right to refuse the search.
In principle no person have to leave the vehicle during the control. With criminal ones have over WantedLevel 2, the government authority can pull those out the vehicle.
The assignment of a Wanted because of refusal is not valid anymore.

Policemen are authorizedto take driving licences/Licenses with repeated offence against valid rules, however only when they informed a Admin.

Repeated controlling one behind the other is forbidden. Between control of the same person must be a period of 20 minutes.


6.2 Purposeful control   


If a vehicle is however stopped, because it offended against one or more traffic rules, additionally a ticket may be assigned. However is valid here: Only one ticket!
If several policemen give in each case a ticket with the same reason to a person, the suspect is only obligated to pay the first.

See more for this in the section;"Tickets".


7. Properties

Fundamental   

Responsibly: All Units*

When examining, the following must be considered:
To including WantedLevel 2, the driver has the right to refuse the search..


7.1 Weapons   

To carry a gun is a gun license needed.
They may not show the weapons nevertheless publicly except its in the headquater or at home (inclusive. Property)
When may the weapons be removed?
Players are permitted to carry a weapon. When the person shoot on a Policemen or other goverment worker, may the weapons be removed.
The decrease of weapons during a robbery is forbidden!


7.2 Materials   

Materials may be only removed, if the owners in the cells among themselves sells weapons. The possession of materials is not punishable, the weapon sale however is, see " WantedLevels".


7.3 Drugs   

Drugs must be removed immediately, the owner expect a ticket (see " Tickets").
The trade with drugs is punishable, see " WantedLevels".

* The command /frisk is forbidden to members of the NG, become these however witnesses of a weapon or a Drogendeals, or see a player with firearms, must they act immediately as the police departments and the FBI (see " WantedLevel").


7.4 ArmorDrugs   

The decrease of ArmorDrugs is not permissible.
The trade with these drugs is however punishable, see " WantedLevels".


8. Protection of the mayor

Fundamental   

Responsibly: Mainly NG, but naturally are obligated all units to protect the mayor in dangerous situations.

In the direct proximity of the mayor, a person with weapon is sighted, than this must be removed immediately, the owner receives a place reference.

If the weapon is used, see " WantedLevels".


9. The Robtime

Fundamental   

Responsibly: All units*

In the Robtime are all units in use and the WantedLevel scopes of responsibility are waived, so a member of the NG may e.g. arrest a robber with only 2 WantedLevels.

For the moment the Robtime is between 9AM & and 2AM.


9.1 Business, bars etc.   

If someone robs a business, a warning appears at the screen. Unfortunately the location of the business is not indicated.
If the author has been noticed, than follows the assignment of 2 WantedLevels which is automatic provided by Security Cams.


9.2 Gas stations / ATM's   

If someone robs a gas station / atm, a warning appears at the screen. The name of the robber can be found out locally only.
If the author has been noticed, than follows the assignment of 3 WantedLevels which is automatic provided by Security Cams. As a Cop staying there aiming (example with a Sniper for insta headshot) is forbidden, the player needs to start running first!


9.3 Bankrob   

If someone robs a bank, a warning appears at the screen.
If the author has been noticed, than follows the assignment of 8 WantedLevels which is automatic provided by Security Cams. Citizens, who stand before the bank, guarding, are directly treated. Here is valid: Who guards, will be imprisoned.
The decrease of weapons during a robbery is forbidden!
Staying inside of bank while a robber is robbing, or waiting for they finish rob is forbidden!

The place of the happening has to be blocked by the Cops and did not take part fellow players e.g. that want into the bank. Lead them around and protect the rob.

When a player enters the bank, than you have to give a warning. When they not leave are you allowed to shoot.

Special arrangement FBI/NG: Members of the FBI/NG are not authorized to give tickets. At burglars into the NG HQ may be shot after a unique warning. If a member of the FBI/NG e.g. notices a burglar in the PD, the responsible policemen of this department must be contacted.

11. Attacks on persons

11.1 Fighting   

Responsibly: All units*
Notices a policeman, member of FBI or the NG that a citizen attacks another citizen (fists) is the policeman[...] permitted to make the aggressor incompetent to act (e.g. /tazer).

Afterwards the assignment of a ticket follows, more in addition in the section " Tickets" (Special arrangement for the FBI and the NG see further below).

After payment of the ticket, a smart money comes by the policeman, who received the ticket payment.

The height of amounts are up to 2000 $ (see " Tickets"). The victim resists (without firearm!), and so the aggressor make self-defence kills and will not be punished. However the attacker dies, and kills the aggressor after Respawn, this independent crime will be treated.

If the attacked person is killed, the aggressor receives automatically 2 WantedLevels and must do the policeman[...] accompany on the district.
If both persons are with equal standing involved to the fight, both receive a ticket and weapons will be removed. The smart money fails in this case. If a person is killed here, and it happens by accident, like " car Wanted; kills" that should be cleared.

Special arrangement FBI/NG: Members of FBI and the NG are not authorized to give tickets, they will give a wantedlevel, the smart money is not valid here.

When a goverment worker will get attacked: See " WantedLevels".


11.2 Shootings   


If a policeman, member of FBI or the NG notice the fact that a citizen attacks another with a firearm or also only threatened may be assigned immediately with two WantedLevels.

14. Traffic accidents

Fundamental   

Responsibly: All units*

Now and then it occurs that two or several vehicles are complicated into an accident. If this happens on a crossing, without the fact that someone offended against the traffic rules valid at the server does not give it punishments.

Offend however a player against a rule, and e.g. causes an accident, because it was on the wrong side on the way, the drivers of the vehicles taken part in to the accident in each case 300 $ must be paid, this regulation are valid also for policeman, members of FBI and the NG, as long as these were not at the time of the accident in the employment.

You can find all traffic rules by the Instructors, ingame or on the forum.

If a player in a vehicle in the proximity of an arrest holds himself (even if this does not take place yet, e.g. if the police before the bank waits around a robber to arrest), are authorized policemen, members of FBI and the NG for the time being to refer this player of the place of the arrest until this is final.

If the player refuses, the vehicle may be destroyed, the player should however carry no damage after a warning.

If a player constantly donates unrest before or in a HQ, a present policeman may express, a member of FBI or the NG after arrangement with the Leader a place reference.

If the player should not depart nevertheless, that is the use of a firearm allowed, however these shots should serve first only as warning. If the player the direct environment of the HQ' s nevertheless does not leave, may deadly shots be fired.

The message over /gov must be repeated every 3 minutes if a certain area as the prohibited area is explained over new joined players to inform them.

19. WantedLevels

Responsibly: All units*   


Starting from WantedLevel 10 firing permission is given, except the suspect places themselves automatically with /handsup and obeys the instructions without to hesitating. If he should not do this, firing permission without further warning is again given.

In principle a WantedLevel may be assigned for each criminal offence, for which a ticket is the standard punishment, if the author flees or the ticket clearly refused. Around Wanteds to distribute the author must be in the periphery of 250 meters.

Should the suspect get too much wantedlevel(s) assigned, than the suspect must become immediately cleared and the legal Wanteds refreshed.

In addition when assigning WantedLevels is to be considered:

Wanteds must be assigned directly with the statement of the crime.
